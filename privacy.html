Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 23, 2025

VisualTennis ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Videos and Media

When you use VisualTennis, you grant the app access to:

  • Videos from your Photos library: Only videos you explicitly select for analysis
  • Video processing: All video processing happens locally on your device
  • Generated content: Analysis results, thumbnails, and processed video clips created by the app

Important: Your videos are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party services. All processing occurs on your device.

1.2 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information, including:

  • Device model and manufacturer
  • Operating system version (iOS version)
  • App version
  • Device identifiers (for crash reporting only)

1.3 Usage Analytics

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, including:

  • Features used and frequency of use
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • Time spent in the app

This data is anonymized and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Provide core functionality: Process and analyze your tennis videos locally on your device
  • Improve the app: Understand how users interact with features and identify areas for improvement
  • Fix bugs: Diagnose and resolve technical issues and crashes
  • Enhance user experience: Optimize performance and develop new features
  • Customer support: Respond to your questions and provide technical assistance

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All your videos and analysis results are stored locally on your device in:

  • Documents directory: Analysis results and clip metadata
  • Caches directory: Thumbnails and temporary files
  • Your Photos library: Original videos remain in your Photos app

3.2 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data is encrypted using iOS built-in encryption
  • Videos never leave your device
  • No cloud storage or server uploads
  • No user accounts or authentication required

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

We may share anonymous, aggregated analytics with:

  • Analytics providers: To understand app usage patterns (data is anonymized)
  • Crash reporting services: To identify and fix bugs (no personal information included)

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Access and Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete data: Clear cache and analysis results through Settings → Storage Management
  • Remove videos: Delete videos from your Photos library to remove them from the app
  • Revoke access: Disable Photos access in iOS Settings → Privacy → Photos
  • Uninstall: Deleting the app removes all locally stored data

5.2 Analytics Opt-Out

While we only collect anonymous analytics, you can limit data collection:

  • iOS Settings → Privacy → Analytics & Improvements → Disable "Share iPhone Analytics"
  • This affects all apps, not just VisualTennis

6. Children's Privacy

VisualTennis is not directed at children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Data Retention

We retain data as follows:

  • Videos and analysis: Stored on your device until you delete them
  • Crash reports: Retained for 90 days for debugging purposes
  • Anonymous analytics: Aggregated data retained indefinitely (cannot identify individual users)

8. International Users

VisualTennis is designed to work entirely offline and locally on your device. However, if you contact us via email:

  • Your email may be stored on servers in various jurisdictions
  • We comply with GDPR for users in the European Union
  • We comply with CCPA for users in California

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • In-app notification (for significant changes)

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Know whether we sell or disclose your personal information
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of your personal information (we do not sell data)

11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent

12. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@visual.tennis

Response time: We aim to respond within 48 hours
